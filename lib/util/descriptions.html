Blessings

In the TST Project, we define a blessing as a short prose, regularly formulaic phrase, often written in the margins, which is meant to attract auspiciousness, but can also express a credo or reverence to a god or a spiritual master.

Colophons

In the TST Project, we define a colophon as whatever text that comes after the end of the work (root-text or commentary), that is, a colophon may include a text completion statement, blessings, the colophon proper (that we call and tag copy statement), a phalaśruti (statement of benefits to the reader, listener; which we call and tag commenting note), etc.

Headers

In the TST Project, we define a header as the left-marginal paratext that appears on page 1 of a work or of a part of a work. It may contain blessings, title or intertitle, author's name, etc.

Invocations

In the TST Project, we define an invocation as a metrical verse, often preceding a text.
